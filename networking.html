Networking & Connections

Build Your Connection at UMSI

UMSI provides a community for people to connect with each other. At UMSI, you gain the opportunity to meet people with different backgrounds. The UMSI community also allows you to engage with UMSI alumni.

Why Connect?

  • Increase Job Opportunities: Enhance your chance of landing a job through referrals.
  • Gain Mentorship: Receive advice and support from UMSI alumni.
  • Acquire Professional Skills: Develop more skills from people with different beackgrounds.
  • Build Relationships: Make friends with other people.
