Career Exploration

Explore Your Career at UMSI

At UMSI, there are career fairs that provide more job opportunities. In addition, there are career coaches at the UMSI Career Development Office that help students reach their career goals. Students can also find out about events through UMSI CareerLink.

How to Develop Your Career

  • Attend Career Fairs: Participating in career fairs allows you to connect with people in the related field.
  • Join Coaching Sessions: Attend coaching sessions hosted by the UMSI Career Development Office to strengthen your career skills.
  • Use UMSI CareerLink: Search for jobs and internships through UMSI CareerLink.
  • Connect with Others: Build relationships with others at UMSI to discover potential job opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Handshake?
    Handshake is a platform that allows people to find and apply for jobs and internships.
  • How do I prepare for interviews?
    The UMSI Career Development Office provides coaching sessions that help people practice interviewing.
  • Where can I attend Career Fairs?
    Information about career fairs can be found at the University Career Center's Career Fair page.
